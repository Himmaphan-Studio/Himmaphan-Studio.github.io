Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

HS is an indie game studio, based online. We are planning to develop a universe that provides with a brand new experience of immersion into a new reality, consisting of games, novels, music and other products. Our studio is to be opened in June 2021 and first products to be released in August.

Our main values is cosmopolicy and internationalisation, meaning uniting people from all over the world. However, we also seek for new approaches to game development.

Our Values

Internationalisation

Our company policy is targeted towards collaborating and employing people from all over the world. We believe that multicultural society is able to create something unique.

New Approach

Our approach consists in not just creating a game, but creating a whole universe with a major plot, where our products are then being developped.

Sustainable Development

We tend to follow sustainable development goals, mainly responsible consumption and production, decent work and reduced inequalities.
